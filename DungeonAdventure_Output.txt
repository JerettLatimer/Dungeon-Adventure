In this Dungeon Adventure game, you pick one of three Heros: A Warrior, Sorcerress, and a Thief.
The chosen Hero will be thrown into a dangerous dungeon containing Monsters and Pits.
The Hero is tasked with locating, retrieving, and safely extracting the Four Pillars of OO.

How To Play Selected

Your selected hero will be dropped into the dungeon. The room your hero is currently in
will be displayed. You can either move your Hero, or use a potion. When you enter a room you will
automatically iteract with whatever is located within the room. Your goal is to visit the 4 rooms
containing the Pillars of OO and safely make it to the exit.
